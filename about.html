More before you get started


Stirling golf community gives golfers the opportunity to try out different courses and play with different people than they would playing their weekly Saturday medal. Playing with members of different courses allows for them to play at discounted rates. The way we arrange pairings gives people the opportunity to learn from better players and teach improving players.


Come and be a part of Stirling’s newest golf social group and enjoy 3 beautiful courses:


Stirling Golf Club – Mondays 6.30pm


Set against the inspiring backdrop of the Old Town of Stirling, built on ancient volcanic slopes, and crowned by the historic Castle rising above the spectacular crags. There are stunning views from the course in all directions across Central Scotland – the Ochil and Lomond Hills to the east, the Carse of Stirling, the Trossachs and the Southern Highlands beyond. More info here.


Dunblane Golf Club – Wednesdays 7.00pm


Our course was designed and laid out by James Braid in conjunction with Major Cecil Hutchison and is constructed on parkland with excellent turf giving it some of the finest fairways around. More info here.


Bridge of Allan Golf Club – Fridays 6.30pm


A nine-hole golf course which requires a climb on several holes, but the golfer is rewarded with spectacular views to Ben Lomond, the Trossachs, the Forth Valley, the Ochil Hills and more locally, of Stirling Castle. More info here.


Sitlring Golf Club – Sundays 11.00am


Sundays are for relaxing! That's why we have a short game hour to work on anything that went wrong in the medal and to catch up with the rest of the group and see how they got on.
